Clubs & Extracurriculars

Coffee & Convo

This is a club that occurs before school rather than after school. Everyone meets up to drink provided coffee and just relax and talk to each other.

FBLA

In this club, students compete with individuals from other schools in business related competitions.

DECA

Similar to FBLA, students in this club compete in business-related competitions against sdtudents from other schools.

Sewing Club

Students in this club meet after school to work on sewing projects. They must bring their own materials.

Book Club

This isn't your average book club! After school, students discuss their favorite books and other literature related topics.

Catholic Breakfast Club

Students meet up before school to enjoy coffee, donuts, and prayer.

Sports

Central has many sports to choose from, such as volleyball, football, soccer, tennis, swimming, basketball, baseball, and softball.

French Club

Students share their love of French language and culture through exploring classic French recipes.

Spanish Club

Students share their love of Spanish language and culture through exploring classic Spanish recipes.

Chess Club

Students meet after school to play chess together.

History Club

Students meet after school to discuss history and current events.

Gaming Club

Students meet after school to discuss current and retro video games and play their favorite games together.
